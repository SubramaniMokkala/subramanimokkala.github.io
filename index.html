About Me

I'm a recent B.Tech graduate specializing in Data Science from KG Reddy College of Engineering & Technology, affiliated with JNTUH. My journey in data science began with a fascination for how satellite imagery could predict agricultural outcomes and has evolved into developing machine learning solutions for climate adaptation.

Throughout my academic career, I've worked on interdisciplinary projects ranging from agricultural technology to healthcare analytics. My major project, "Predictive Modeling of Non-Communicable Diseases Using Machine Learning," was published at ICMED 2025, demonstrating my ability to apply ML in high-stakes domains.

Currently, I'm focused on climate risk assessment using satellite data and machine learning, with the goal of making early warning systems accessible to vulnerable communities. I'm pursuing a Master's degree to deepen my expertise in advanced ML techniques and climate science.
